Who am I?

Currently, I am due to complete my Network Computing degree at Oxford Brookes University in May and graduate in June. After falling in love with programming a few years ago, it quickly became my passion and my hobby. Since then I have been programming religiously, always with an ongoing personal project. I was fortunate enough to obtain an internship at Oxford Web, and ended up working there for 14 months. It was at Oxford Web that I realized how much there was to learn; this fueled my enthusiasm and I have been striving to grow and improve ever since.
